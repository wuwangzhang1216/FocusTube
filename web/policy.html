Privacy Policy for FocusTube

Last Updated: March 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how FocusTube ("we," "our," or "us") handles user data and privacy while you use our browser extension.

Data Collection and Usage

  • No Personal Data Collection: FocusTube does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or identifiable user information.
  • Local Storage: The extension uses your browser's local storage solely to save your settings and preferences. This information remains strictly within your device and is never sent externally or shared with third parties.

Permissions

  • Host Permissions: FocusTube requests permissions only for YouTube (*://*.youtube.com/*) to detect and skip video advertisements automatically. The extension does not interact with or access data from any other websites.
  • Scripting Permission: Required solely to inject necessary scripts on YouTube pages for detecting and skipping ads.

Security

We are committed to ensuring your privacy and security. Since we do not collect personal data or track user behavior, your privacy remains fully protected.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be updated on this page. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
